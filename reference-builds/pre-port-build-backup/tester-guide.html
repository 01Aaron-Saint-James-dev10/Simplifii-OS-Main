Simplifii-OS

A free thinking tool for students. It helps you decode assignments, organise your work, and check your claims before you submit. It does not write for you. It works with you.

4 things to try

1

Sign up

Go to simplifii-os-main.vercel.app/signup. Pick your year level. Set up your workspace.

2

Upload an assessment

Drop in a brief, syllabus, or course outline PDF. We will extract your assessments and due dates automatically.

3

Ask the tutor

Open the Tutor panel in the editor. Ask it anything about your assessment. It asks you questions to sharpen your thinking.

4

Try voice input

Click the mic button in the editor. Speak your thoughts. Text appears at the cursor. Works best in Chrome.

Found a bug or have an idea? Tap the green feedback button in the bottom-right corner of any screen. Every message is read by the founder personally.

You can also email aaron@simplifii.com.au directly.

If you need support, there is a "Talk to someone" link on every screen with verified crisis contacts including Kids Helpline (1800 55 1800), QLife (1800 184 527), and Lifeline (13 11 14).
