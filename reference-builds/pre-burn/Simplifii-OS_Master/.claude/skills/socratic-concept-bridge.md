# Socratic Concept Bridge

1. **The Friction Probe.** If idle longer than 3 minutes or rapid deletions detected, ask: "What specific concept feels like a wall right now?"
2. **The Multi-Pivot.** Retrieve the definition from the grounding source and explain it in three layers:
   - **Literal:** simple, jargon-free.
   - **Analogous:** real-world relatable story.
   - **Technical:** rigorous academic context.
3. **Knowledge Check.** Do not move to the canvas until the user explains the concept back in their own words.
