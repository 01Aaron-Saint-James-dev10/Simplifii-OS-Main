# LOD Compass Scaffolder

1. **Compass Mode.** By default, show only ONE active micro-step. Hide the rest of the Map.
2. **Progressive Reveal.** Only show Envelope 2 once Envelope 1 has 4 or more section health dots.
3. **Complexity Steering.** Adjust the academic density of suggestions based on the user's `stream_id` (Primary through Tertiary).
