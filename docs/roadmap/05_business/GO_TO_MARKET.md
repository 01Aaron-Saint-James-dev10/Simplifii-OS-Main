# Go-to-Market Sequence [SPEC]

## What this is

The order and method of getting Sovereign OS into market. Which mode launches first, who hears about it, how trust is built, when B2B begins.

## Status

[SPEC] — sequence proposed, needs Aaron's strategic input.

## Strategic logic

You don't launch all 7 modes at once. That dilutes brand, splits attention, burns capital.

You launch ONE mode brilliantly, build proof, expand to adjacent modes, then turn on B2B.

## Risk management

### Risk: Burnout
Aaron building alone is unsustainable. Mitigation: focus on Phase 1 only until $30K MRR, then hire.

### Risk: Cash runway
Bootstrap until paying customers. Mitigation: founder pricing locks in early MRR before raising. Consider Boost Camp / Antler / OneVentures angel networks after Phase 1 traction.

### Risk: Feature creep
Backlog explodes faster than build. Mitigation: this roadmap exists. Each sprint = one feature.

### Risk: Anthropic dependency
Currently using Claude AI under the hood. Mitigation: prompt-engineer for portability. Already capable of swapping to local LLM (Ollama, llama.cpp) or other providers (OpenAI, Gemini) within 1 week.

### Risk: Institutional buying cycle
Universities buy slowly. 6-12 month sales cycles. Mitigation: Phase 4 starts in March 2027 because Year 1 of B2B = relationship building, not contracts. Real B2B revenue starts year 2.
