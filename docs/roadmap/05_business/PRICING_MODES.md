# Pricing Modes [SPEC]

## What this is

The pricing structure across all Sovereign OS modes. Different tiers, different prices, different go-to-market. This is the business model document, not code.

## Status

[SPEC] — needs Aaron's strategic input on actual numbers before any sales begin.

## Pricing philosophy

1. **Privacy-first stays free at the foundation level.** Local-first storage, no ads, no data sales. The mission demands free or near-free baseline access.
2. **Premium features pay for premium infrastructure.** Practice mode video processing, AI ingestion at scale, Receipt verification — these cost real money to run, justified premium tiers.
3. **Equity tiers exist.** Scholarship-funded, hardship-verified free tiers across all modes.
4. **B2B is the scale engine.** Institutional contracts cross-subsidise individual access.
5. **NDIS-fundable where applicable.** Maths Autistic, Homeschool, neurodivergent support → pursue Capacity Building - Improved Learning eligibility.
6. **No surprise charges. No data harvesting. Ever.** This is the moat.

## Yearly discounts

- 10% off for yearly upfront
- 15% off for 2-year commitment
- No discount for monthly (keeps friction)

## Founder pricing (early adopters)

First 1,000 paying users get permanent grandfathered pricing:
- Standard tier locked at $15/month forever (vs $25 future)
- Research tier locked at $29/month forever (vs $79 future)
- Builds early loyalty + community
- Word-of-mouth marketing

## Refund policy

- 30-day no-questions refund
- Pause subscription up to 3 months/year
- Cancel any time, retain data export rights

## Payment infrastructure

- Stripe for cards
- PayPal supported
- Crypto eventually (USDC, BTC)
- BPAY for AU institutional
- Direct invoice for B2B
- No auto-renewal surprise (clear renewal email 7 days before)

## Pricing psychology

- $15-25 sweet spot for students: matches Spotify/Netflix
- $39-79 sweet spot for researchers: matches Zotero pro / Notion pro
- $89-149 sweet spot for academics: matches enterprise productivity
- $59 family: matches Disney+ family / education subscriptions
- $25K-300K university: matches mid-tier vendor contracts

All below the "expense report friction" thresholds in each segment.

## Anti-patterns we will NOT do

- ❌ Freemium with crippling limits (the free tier must be genuinely useful)
- ❌ Surprise add-ons (everything in the tier is in the tier)
- ❌ Data harvesting in exchange for free access
- ❌ Ads, ever
- ❌ Selling user data, ever
- ❌ Per-feature unlocks ("pay $5 to use Receipt this month") — that's value extraction not partnership
- ❌ Pricing changes for existing customers without notice
- ❌ Hidden cancellation friction
- ❌ Marketing emails the user didn't ask for
