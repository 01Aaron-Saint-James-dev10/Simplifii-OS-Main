## How to leverage relationships ETHICALLY

### Always
- Be transparent about Sovereign as a separate venture from advisory work
- Disclose conflicts (Aaron as MRes researcher AND Sovereign founder)
- Offer reciprocal value (Aaron's advisory work helps these orgs; Sovereign separate)
- Respect "no" — not everyone wants to be sales fodder

### Never
- Use advisory positions to push Sovereign sales
- Imply institutional endorsement when none exists
- Trade insider information for personal venture gain
- Approach relationships transactionally only

### The right framing

When introducing Sovereign:
- "I'm building this in parallel to my MRes / advisory work"
- "I'd love your honest feedback, no pressure to engage"
- "If it's useful to your community, I'd be honoured to support that"
- Not: "Hey can you give me a sales intro / endorsement / pilot"
