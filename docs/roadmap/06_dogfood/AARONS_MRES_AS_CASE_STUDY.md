# Aaron's MRes as Sovereign Case Study [SPEC]

## What this is

Aaron's MRes (and trajectory into PhD and beyond) IS the dogfood. Every chapter, every methodology decision, every reflexive note is written in Sovereign. This is the strongest founder narrative possible.

## Status

[SPEC] — already happening. Formalise the strategy.

## Why this matters

### For the product
Aaron is the user. Aaron knows what's missing because he hits the wall daily. Feature priorities come from his real friction, not theoretical user research. Building products you use yourself is the strongest possible product strategy.

### For the marketing
"Built by an Australian MRes researcher, while writing his MRes" is more compelling than any feature list. Founders who eat their own dog food in public build trust faster than any ad campaign.

### For the research
Aaron's MRes thesis becomes the methodologically documented case study of Sovereign's effectiveness for neurodivergent academic users. Phase 2 PhD work extends this to disability-led co-production research on the product itself.

### For credibility
Universities buying Sovereign want evidence. Aaron's MRes IS evidence. Watching the receipt accumulate over 18 months across hundreds of sessions = the most defensible proof of authenticity any tool has ever delivered.

## Three-phase research integration

### Phase 1: MRes (2026-2027) — Institutional Mapping
- **Strand 1:** National Policy and Resource Audit (38-42 Australian universities)
- **Strand 2:** UNSW staff survey + cross-institution interviews
- **Sovereign integration:** EVERY chapter written in Sovereign. Methodology log captures real pivots (e.g. SA university merger reducing audit scope). Reflexivity log captures dual-role tensions (Aaron co-developed some evaluated resources at UNSW). Receipt generated at submission.

### Phase 2: PhD (2027-2030) — Disability-Led Co-Production
- **Strand 1:** Lived experience co-research with neurodivergent researchers using Sovereign
- **Strand 2:** Implementation case studies at HIGH-adoption institutions (Sovereign is one of the implementations)
- **Strand 3:** Framework development (UDL 3.0+ enhanced through Sovereign use)
- **Sovereign integration:** This becomes literal evaluation research on Sovereign. The PhD validates the product. Beautiful loop.

### Phase 3: Academic Position (2030+) — Sector Transformation
- **Continuing research:** Sovereign at national scale; sector evaluation
- **Sovereign integration:** Aaron remains the user-in-chief. Product-research feedback loop continues.

## Risks

### Risk: Public dogfooding exposes thesis IP
Mitigation: share process, not findings prematurely. Methodology and approach are public; specific findings remain confidential until publication.

### Risk: Supervisor relationship tension
Mitigation: Prof Cumming aware of Sovereign. Conversation about boundaries / disclosure already had. Document the agreement.

### Risk: UNSW reflexivity issue (Aaron co-developed evaluated UDL resources)
Mitigation: this is a known thesis methodological tension. Sovereign's Reflexivity Log helps document it transparently. Discuss explicitly in Chapter 1 / Methodology.

### Risk: Founder narrative becomes prescriptive
"Aaron does it this way; you should too" is not the message. Sovereign supports many cognitive styles. Aaron is one user. Mitigation: feature other users' stories prominently.

### Risk: MRes delayed by Sovereign building
Mitigation: this entire roadmap exists. Discipline = thesis ships. Sprint priority queue protects thesis time.
