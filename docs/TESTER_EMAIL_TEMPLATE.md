Hi [Name],

I'm Aaron, the founder of Simplifii-OS. I'm inviting you to be one of the first people to test it.

**What it is:**
Simplifii-OS is a free thinking tool for students. It helps you decode assignment briefs, organise your work, and check your claims before you submit. It does not write for you. It works with you.

**What to try (15 minutes is plenty):**
1. Sign up at https://simplifii-os-main.vercel.app/signup
2. Upload an assignment brief or syllabus PDF
3. Ask the Socratic tutor a question about your assessment
4. Try voice input (click the mic button in the editor, Chrome works best)

**How to give feedback:**
There is a green feedback button in the bottom-right corner of every screen. Tap it, pick Bug or Idea, and tell us what happened. Every message is read by me personally.

**What to expect:**
This is a beta. Some features are still being built. AI responses are real (powered by Claude) but may not always be perfect. Your data stays yours and is stored encrypted in Sydney.

**If you need support:**
There is a "Talk to someone" link on every screen with verified crisis support contacts, including Kids Helpline, QLife, and Lifeline.

**Privacy:**
Your work is private. We do not share individual data with anyone. You can delete your account at any time.

Thank you for helping us build something better.
