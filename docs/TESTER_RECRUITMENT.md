# Tester Recruitment Plan

## Target
10-20 Year 10-12 students. Mixed schools. Neurodivergent learners welcome but not required.

## Where to find them
1. **Aria Learning students** (if Abby consents to Aaron reaching out via her network)
2. **Aaron's personal network** (UNSW education contacts, former tutees)
3. **NSW homeschool networks** (Facebook groups, with parental consent)
4. **Reddit r/HSC** (post with mod approval, link to signup)
5. **Discord HSC servers** (with admin permission)
6. **Neurodivergent youth networks** (with safeguarding: always include parent/guardian consent)

## Privacy expectations briefing

Tell every tester (and parent, for under-18s):
- Your work is private. We do not share it.
- Your data is encrypted and stored in Sydney.
- You can export or delete your data at any time.
- We do not sell data or use it for advertising.
- Crisis support is always available in the app.

## Feedback expectations

- 15 minutes of use is plenty
- Use the in-app feedback button (green, bottom-right)
- Pick "Bug" for things that broke, "Idea" for suggestions
- Every message is read by Aaron personally
- No formal report needed

## Reward

None. This is voluntary beta testing. Be transparent about this.
Do not offer gift cards, credits, or incentives.
Do not imply future benefits in exchange for testing.
